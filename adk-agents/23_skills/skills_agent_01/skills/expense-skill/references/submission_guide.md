# How to Submit an Expense Claim

## Step-by-step Process

1. **Collect receipts** — Keep all original receipts. Photos of receipts are accepted.
2. **Log in to the expense portal** — Go to `expenses.company.com` and click "New Claim".
3. **Select expense category** — Choose from the dropdown (Meals, Travel, Supplies, etc.).
4. **Enter the amount** — Enter the exact amount in the currency you paid in.
5. **Attach receipt** — Upload a clear photo or scan of the receipt.
6. **Add a business justification** — Briefly explain the business purpose (e.g., "Client lunch with Acme Corp to discuss Q2 contract").
7. **Submit for approval** — Claims go to your direct manager automatically.
8. **Track status** — Check the portal for approval status. You will also get an email notification.

## Deadlines

- Submit within **30 days** of the expense date.
- Late submissions (31–60 days) require manager justification.
- Claims older than **60 days** are not accepted.

## Reimbursement Timeline

- Approved claims are processed in the **next payroll cycle** (bi-weekly).
- International claims may take one additional cycle due to currency conversion.

## Common Rejection Reasons

- Missing or unreadable receipt
- No business justification provided
- Expense exceeds policy limit without pre-approval
- Submission after the 30-day deadline
